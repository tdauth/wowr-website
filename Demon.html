The demon race is a mixture of Eredar, Fel Beasts, Fel Guards, Doom Guards, Pit Lords and other creatures. It is the Burning Legion which tries conquering other worlds and originates from Argus. It is a new race which was not provided by Warcraft III: Reforged.
