Your first hero can be chosen in the beginning and is your main unit who can gain experience from killing enemy units. Gaining experience will lead to leveling up. The maximum hero level is 75. Heroes have 3 different attributes: Strength, Agility and Intelligence which all increase certain stats. One of the 3 attributes is the hero'S primary attribute which also increases the caused damage by the hero. For every level up a hero gains additional attributes and 1 skill point which can be spent on one of the hero spells improving its effect. The primary attribute, start attributes, attributes per level and spells differ from hero to hero.
