Your first hero can be chosen in the beginning and is your main unit who can gain experience from killing enemy units. Gaining experience will lead to leveling up. The maximum hero level is 75. Heroes have 3 different attributes:
  • Strength: Increases maximum hit points and hit points regeneration.
  • Agility: Increases the armor, attack and movement speed.
  • Intelligence: Increases the maximum mana and man regeneration.
One of the 3 attributes is the hero's primary attribute which also increases the caused damage by the hero. For every level up a hero gains additional attributes and 1 skill point which can be spent on one of the hero spells improving its effect. The primary attribute, start attributes, attributes per level and spells differ from hero to hero. You can pick up to 3 heroes in World of Warcraft Reforged depending on your highest hero level (see Hero Journey). All 3 heroes can be saved and loaded. It stores only their gained experience, so you have to reskill them in every game. Besides, bonuses by tomes will be lost.
