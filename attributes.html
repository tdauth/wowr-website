Heroes can skill attributes:
Icon Name Description
Strength Increases maximum hit points and hit points regeneration.
Agility Increases the armor and attack speed.
Intelligence Increases the maximum mana and mana regeneration.
Attribute Points Allow skilling the standard attributes for your hero.
Instinct Increases the hero's sight and movement by 10 per point.
Stamina Temporarily increases the movement and attack speed of your hero.
Luck Increases the chance for positive events for your hero.
Charisma Increases the chance to persuade other characters for your hero.
Mystic Increases the hero's magic resistance by 3 and reduces the cooldown by 0.2 seconds per point.
Thieving Increases the chance to rob units and to loot chests.
