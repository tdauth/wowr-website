Dig Holy Grail
Unique

The Holy Grail.

Increases your your collected gold and harvested lumber by 10. All Computer players lose 50 % of their collected gold and harvested lumber to you. Reduces build and upgrade time of your structures to zero. Gives all of your units the Divine armor type.

Requires 9 Grail Puzzle Pieces and there can be only 1 Holy Grail in the game. Use the chat command "-pingo" to ping all Obelisks on the map.

Craft Orb of Orbs
Combines effects of known orbs to one single item. Adds 60 bonus damage to the attack of a Hero when carried. The Hero's attack also becomes ranged when attacking air and it has the following effects:
- AOE damage.
- Slows movement speed.
- Summons a Dark Minion on the unit's death.
- Slows attack rate.
- Decreases armor.
- Poisons the target.
- Chance to dispel magic and dealing extra damage to summoned units.
- Resurrects up to 6 dead organic player units.
- Destroys 100 % mana of the caused damage.
- Deals 5 % extra damage against Undead units.
- Chance of 20 % to charm enemy units with a base level up to 5.
- Give a chance of 30 % to web enemy units for 12 seconds.
- Restores 5 % of the caused damage as hit points.
- Reduce the enemy's attack damage by 5 % for 5 seconds.

Requires 6 Orbs.
