Use the chat command "-unlock" to disable the hero level requirements for anything. The highest level of your heroes determines which features are available for you:
  • 5: Abilities available on Theramore.
  • 10: -
  • 15: One Equipment Bag.
  • 20: Mount.
  • 25: Second profession.
  • 30: Bonus races.
  • 35: Divine Golems.
  • 40: Bonus heroes and second equipment bag.
  • 45: Second hero and third profession.
  • 50: Boss heroes.
  • 55: Bonus mounts.
  • 60: Second race and third equipment bag.
  • 65: Third hero and third race.
  • 70: AI for yourself.
  • 75: Void Lord can be chosen as hero who can learn different spells.
  • 75: Advanced chat commands from '-helpvisual' can be used.
  • 75 (all 3 heroes): All races, professions and chat command '-maxbosslevels' are available.
  • Donating/Reporting bugs: VIP.
