Wool 1.20 - Wool can be used for clothing.
Rocks 0.40 - Rocks can be used to construct stone buildings and be used as ammunition for catapults.
Iron 0.50 - Iron is used for weapons and armor.
Black Powder 0.80 - Black Powder is used to fire guns and cannons.
Water 0.20 - Water is gained from rivers, seas and wells. It is used to make food grow, extinguish fire and to supply your units.
Power 1.30 - The power source for power generators and electricity.
Favor 1.10 - Favor is given by the gods and titans. It is gained by sacrifing animals, fighting wars, praying and building monuments.
Fruits 0.40 - Fruits include all fruits and vegetables but not grain.
Fel 2.00 - A demonic, destructive form of magic which leads to corruption of the target.
Argunite 2.00 - Mineral from Argus with magical properties.
