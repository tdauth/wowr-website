Resources can be gathered and exchanged into gold. The resulting gold depends on their gold exchange rate:
Icon Name Gold Exchange Rate Tax Levels Description
Gold 1.0
  • 0-50 Food: 100% income
  • 51-80 Food: 70% income
  • 81-100 Food: 40% income
 Gold is the main resource of Azeroth. It can be gathered from Gold Mines. It is used to buy items, train units, research upgrades and construct buildings.
Lumber 2.0 - Lumber is the second most important resource of Azeroth. It can be gathered from trees. It is used for more expensive stuff since it might take longer to gather lumber than gold.
Food - - Food is used by player units. Stronger units use more food.
Food Maximum - - The food maximum is determined by the farms/burrows etc. you have constructed and limited by 600.
Oil 2.0 - Oil can be gathered from Oil Fields on land or water. It is burned as fuel for mechanical vehicles.
Copper 0.25 - Copper is the cheapest currency of Azeroth. 2 Copper is worth 1 silver. 4 Copper is worth 1 gold. 2 Silver are worth 1 gold.
Silver 0.50 - Silver is the second cheapest currency of Azeroth. It is worth more than Copper but less than Gold. 2 Copper is worth 1 silver. 4 Copper is worth 1 gold. 2 Silver are worth 1 gold.
Gemstones 2.50 - Gemstones is the most valuable resource in Azeroth which can be harvested.
Food 0.40 - Food is used to feed your units and animals. It includes all different sorts of food like grain, meat, fruits etc.
Rocks 0.40 - Rocks can be used to construct stone buildings and be used as ammunition for catapults.
Iron 0.50 - Iron is used for weapons and armor.
Black Powder 0.80 - Black Powder is used to fire guns and cannons.
Leather 0.60 - Leather is used for clothing and armor.
Water 0.60 - Water is gained from rivers, seas and wells. It is used to make food grow, extinguish fire and to supply your units.
Mana 0.30 - Mana is used to cast spells.
Electricity 1.30 - Electricity is unknown in Azeroth but known on earth.
Favor 1.20 - Favor is given by the gods and titans. It is gained by sacrifing animals, fighting wars, praying and building monuments.
Beer 0.80 - The essential drink for the Pandaren race and many more to endure this cruel world.
