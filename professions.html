Professions allow you to craft items. The first profession has to be chosen in the beginning of the game.
Icon Name Description Item
Herbalist Allows crafting items which heal your hero and increase the life. Book of Herbalism
Alchemist Allows crafting items which restore mana of your hero and increase the maximum mana. Book of Alchemy
Weapon Smith Allows crafting items which increase the damage of your hero. Book of Weaponsmithing
Armorer Allows crafting items which increase the armor of your hero. Book of Armoring
Engineer Allows crafting tiny towers to protect your base. Book of Engineering
Lore Master Allows crafting scrolls. Book of Lores
Sorcerer Allows crafting wands. Book of Sorcery
Runeforger Allows crafting runes. Book of Runeforging
Dragon Breeder Allows crafting dragon eggs. Book of Dragon Breeder
Jewelcrafter Allows crafting items which increase your hero attributes. Book of Jewelcrafting
Enchanter Allows crafting items which increase the bonuses of carried items. Recommended as secondary profession since it helps to improve other items. Use "-enchanter" to list all item types which can be enchanted. Book of Enchanting
Pirate Allows crafting items which allow you to live as a pirate. Arrr! Book of Piracy
Archaeologist Allows digging for random items. Book of Archeology
Witch Doctor Allows crafting wards. Book of Witch Doctor
Merchant Allows crafting bills to increase charges of other items. Book of Merchant
