Professions allow your hero to craft items. The first profession has to be picked in the beginning of the game. The other two professions can be picked later with a higher hero level. There are the following profession ranks which simply require more mana to craft better items:
  • Novice
  • Advanced
  • Adept
  • Master
  • Grand Master
  • Doctor
Hover the number per item or unit type to see the corresponding rank.
