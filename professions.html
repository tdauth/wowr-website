Professions allow you to craft items. The first profession has to be chosen in the beginning of the game.
Icon Name Crafted Items Item Description
Herbalist
Craft items which heal your hero and increase the hero's life:
- Potion of Greater Healing
- Health Stone
- Potion of Invulnerability
- Talisman of Everlasting
Alchemist
Craft items which restore mana of your hero and increase the hero's maximum mana:
- Potion of Greater Mana
- Mana Stone
- Wand of Mana Stealing
- Talisman of Spell Protection
Weapon Smith
Craft items which increase the damage of your hero:
- Bow of Fire
- Mithril Long Sword
- Demon Slayer Axe
- Blessed Dragon Lance
Armorer
Craft items which increase the armor of your hero:
- Light Leather Armor
- Plated Helmet
- Heavy Plated Shield
- Blessed Champion Armor
Engineer
Crafting tiny towers to protect your base:
- Tiny Flame Tower
- Tiny Cold Tower
- Sapper
- Tiny Death Tower
Lore Master
Crafting items with temporary effects:
- Scroll of Protection
- Scroll of Restoration
- Scroll of Resurrection
- Ankh of Reincarnation
Sorcerer
Crafting wands with temporary effects and multiple charges:
- Wand of Neutralization
- Wand of the Wind
- Wand of Reanimation
- Wand of Sorcerer Illusion
Runeforger
Craft runes with multiple charges:
- Rune of Speed
- Rune of Dispel Magic
- Rune of Restoration
- Rune of Rebirth
Dragon Breeder
Craft dragon eggs:
- Green Dragon Whelp Egg
- Tharifas Egg
- Green Drake Egg
- Green Dragon Egg
Jewelcrafter
Craft items which increase your hero attributes:
- Ring of Superiority
- Enchanted Gemstone
- Green Gemstone
- Jewel Amulet
Enchanter
Crafting formulas which increase the bonuses of carried items. Recommended as secondary profession since it helps to improve other items. Use "-enchanter" to list all item types which can be enchanted.
- Novice Enchanting Formula
- Advanced Enchanting Formula
- Adept Enchanting Formula
- Master Enchanting Formula
Pirate
Craft items which allow you to live as a pirate. Arrr!
- Tiny Pirate Ship
- Treasure Map
- Compass
- Navigation Wheel
Archaeologist
Allows digging for random items:
- Random Item Level 1
- Random Item Level 3
- Random Item Level 5
- Random Item Level 7
Witch Doctor
Craft wards:
- Sentry Wards
- Stasis Traps
- Mana Wards
- Healing Wards
Merchant
Craft bills to increase charges of other items:
- Small Bill
- Bill
- Large Bill
- Ring of the Trading Guild
Farmer
Constructs buildings which give you additional food, gold and lumber:
- Farm
- Water Supply
- Granary
- Wind Mill
