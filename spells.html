Spells can be cast by units to achieve some effect in the game which usually helps the owner or allied players. Spells cost mana which is required to cast them. Spells can have multiple levels. Your hero can learn new or level up spells with every level. Units can also have spells. Unit spells are improved with researches. 1 3
