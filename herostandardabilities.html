Every hero can have one single standard ability from start which might have only one level or will be skilled automatically without spending any skill points:
Icon Name Description
Berserk Causes this unit to attack 50% faster, but take 40% more damage.
Invisibility Makes a unit invisible. If the unit attacks, uses an ability or casts a spell, it will become visible.
Lasts 120 seconds.
Magic Sentry Reveals nearby invisible units.
Bloodlust Increases a friendly unit's attack rate by 30% and movement speed by 25%.
Lasts 60 seconds.
Ensnare Causes a target enemy unit to be bound to the ground so that it cannot move for 8 seconds. Air units that are ensnared can be attacked as though they were land units.
True Sight Reveals nearby invisible units.
VIP Spell Book Only VIPs can use this spell book. Contains the spells Blink, Repair, Dark Summoning, Ensnare, Bloodlust, Bash, Berserk, Invisibility, and True Sight.
Blink Teleports the caster a short distance, allowing it to move in and out of combat.
Repair Repairs mechanical units and structures at the cost of resources.
Dark Summoning Summons 12 friendly units from a target area to the caster.
Cyclone Tosses a target non-mechanical enemy unit into the air, rendering it unable to move, attack or cast spells, and stopping others from attacking or casting on it.
Lasts 20 seconds.
Rejuvenation Heals a target friendly unit for 400 hit points over 12 seconds.
Root Burrows the Ancient's roots into the ground, rendering it immobile, but allowing it to build units. For the Ancient Protector, this allows it to hurl enormous rocks through the air to damage enemy units. Also gives the Ancient Fortified armor.
Randomize Randomizes the learned spells of your hero.

Only allowed for heroes who can change their spells. Requires all hero spells to have no cooldown.
Skill Allows skilling hero attributes and randomizing hero spells.
Master and Student Increases the damage and attack speed of Medivh when being cast by Khadgar or of Khadgar when being cast by Medivh.
