Every hero can have one single standard ability from start which might have only one level or will be skilled automatically without spending any skill points:
Icon Name Levels Description
Skill 1 Allows skilling hero attributes and randomizing hero spells.
VIP Spell Book 1 Contains the spells Blink, Repair, Dark Summoning, Ensnare, Bloodlust, Bash, Berserk, Invisibility, and True Sight.

Only allowed for VIPs.
Berserk 15 Causes the hero to attack faster, but take more damage.
Invisibility 15 Makes a unit invisible. If the unit attacks, uses an ability or casts a spell, it will become visible.
Magic Sentry 1 Reveals nearby invisible units.
Bloodlust 15 Increases a friendly unit's attack rate and movement speed.
Ensnare 1 Causes a target enemy unit to be bound to the ground so that it cannot move for some time. Air units that are ensnared can be attacked as though they were land units.
True Sight 1 Changes the ability to True Sight.
Blink 1 Changes the ability to Blink.
Repair 1 Repairs mechanical units and structures at the cost of resources.

Increases the ratio of repaired hit points with every level.
Dark Summoning 1 Summons 12 friendly units from a target area to the caster.
Cyclone 1 Tosses a target non-mechanical enemy unit into the air, rendering it unable to move, attack or cast spells, and stopping others from attacking or casting on it.
Lasts 20 seconds.
Rejuvenation 15 Heals a target friendly unit for hit points over some time.
Ultravision 1 The ability to see as far at night as they do during the day.
Nocturnal 1 Increases stats during the night.
Diurnal 1 Increases stats during the day.
Speed Boost 15 Increases the movement speed for some time.
Summon Ship 1 Summons a ship.
Summon Submarine 1 Summons a submarine.
Enslavement 1 Converts a killed enemy into a Slave at your nearest town hall.
Loot Ray 1 Casts a ray which pulls target items to the hero.
Root 1 Burrows the Ancient's roots into the ground, rendering it immobile, but allowing it to build units. For the Ancient Protector, this allows it to hurl enormous rocks through the air to damage enemy units. Also gives the Ancient Fortified armor.

Only allowed for Ancient structure heroes.
Burrow 1 Digs into the ground, becomes invisible, and gains an increased regeneration rate.
The hero cannot attack while burrowed.

Only allowed for Nerubian and Kobold heroes.
Submerge 1 Allows to dive beneath water, hiding it from view.

Only allowed for Submarine and Naga heroes. It is added automatically when the hero is on water.
Randomize Spells 1 Randomizes the learned spells of your hero.

Only allowed for heroes who can change their spells. Requires all hero spells to have no cooldown.
Randomize Skin 1 Randomizes the skin of your hero.

Only allowed for heroes who can change their skin.
Master and Student 15 Changes the ability to Master and Student.
Select Turrets 1 Changes the ability to Select Turrets.
Spell Book of the Old Gods 1 Contains different spells.

Only allowed for Old God bosses.
