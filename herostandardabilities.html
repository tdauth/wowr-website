Every hero can have one single standard ability from start which might have only one level or will be skilled automatically without spending any skill points:
Icon Name Levels Description
Berserk 15 Causes this unit to attack 50% faster, but take 40% more damage.
Invisibility 15 Makes a unit invisible. If the unit attacks, uses an ability or casts a spell, it will become visible.
Lasts 120 seconds.
Magic Sentry 1 Reveals nearby invisible units.
Bloodlust 15 Increases a friendly unit's attack rate by 30% and movement speed by 25%.
Lasts 60 seconds.
Ensnare 1 Causes a target enemy unit to be bound to the ground so that it cannot move for 8 seconds. Air units that are ensnared can be attacked as though they were land units.
True Sight 1 Reveals nearby invisible units.
VIP Spell Book 1 Only VIPs can use this spell book. Contains the spells Blink, Repair, Dark Summoning, Ensnare, Bloodlust, Bash, Berserk, Invisibility, and True Sight.
Blink 1 Teleports the caster a short distance, allowing it to move in and out of combat.
Repair 1 Repairs mechanical units and structures at the cost of resources.
Dark Summoning 1 Summons 12 friendly units from a target area to the caster.
Cyclone 1 Tosses a target non-mechanical enemy unit into the air, rendering it unable to move, attack or cast spells, and stopping others from attacking or casting on it.
Lasts 20 seconds.
Rejuvenation 1 Heals a target friendly unit for 400 hit points over 12 seconds.
Ultravision 1 The ability to see as far at night as they do during the day.
Speed Boost 15 Increases the movement speed by 60 % for 20 seconds.
Loot Ray 1 Casts a ray which pulls target items to the hero.
Root 1 Burrows the Ancient's roots into the ground, rendering it immobile, but allowing it to build units. For the Ancient Protector, this allows it to hurl enormous rocks through the air to damage enemy units. Also gives the Ancient Fortified armor.
Randomize Spells 1 Randomizes the learned spells of your hero.

Only allowed for heroes who can change their spells. Requires all hero spells to have no cooldown.
Randomize Skin 1 Randomizes the skin of your hero.

Only allowed for heroes who can change their skin.
Skill 1 Allows skilling hero attributes and randomizing hero spells and skins.
Master and Student 15 Increases the damage and attack speed of Medivh when being cast by Khadgar or of Khadgar when being cast by Medivh.
Select Turrets 1 Selects all the turrets of the vehicle. Turrets will attack nearby enemies automatically or attack the focused target while the vehicle can continue its current order. This allows you moving the vehicle while it is attacking. Some turrets can be destroyed without losing the vehicle.
