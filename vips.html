VIPs

Subscribe to become a VIP and support the project.

Reporting bugs and helping the project in other ways can also make you a VIP.

VIPs have many additional features and will benefit more from updates. Send me your Battle.net ID on Discord after subscribing to get it unlocked with the next map release.

Features

You get the following features as a VIP:
  • Save Games
  • Player Housings
  • Access to the VIP room with useful respawning items.
  • Exclusive VIP items.
  • Hidden bases from the start.
  • All heroes and races from the start.
  • All skins.
  • All mounts.
  • High priority for wishes of new features in the map.
  • Possible translation into your native language.
  • VIP status on Discord.
