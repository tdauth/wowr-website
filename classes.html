Increases the stats of the summoned unit with every level.
Witch Doctor

Changes the class to Witch Doctor:
- Shadow Magic
- Weakening
- Charm
- Hex
Witch Doctor
Serpent Ward

Summons an immobile serpentine ward to attack the hero's enemies. The ward is magic and spell immune.
Lasts 40 seconds.

Attacks land and air units.


Increases the stats of the serpentine ward with every level.
Silence

Stops all enemies in a small target area from casting spells for 40 seconds.

Increases the duration with every level.
Slow

Slows a target enemy unit's attack rate by 25% and movement speed by 60%.
Lasts 20 seconds.

Increases the percentages with every level.
Anti-magic Shell

Creates a barrier that stops points of spell damage from affecting a target unit. Causes damage to summoned units. Reduces magic damage taken by the target unit by a percentage.
Lasts 90 seconds.

Increases the damage points stopped, the damage points dealt to summoned unit and the percentage of reduced magic damage with every level.
Hex

Transforms an enemy unit into a random critter for 15 seconds. Hex disables a units abilities, item usage, ability to attack, and slows the unit to minimum movement speed.

Increases the duration with every level.
Scream

Lets loose a terrifying howl that causes nearby enemy units to shiver in fear, reducing their armor by 2.
Lasts 10 seconds.

Decreases the armor with every level.
Ghost Form

Transforms the hero into an invisible Ghost with a ranged attack and 200 bonus hit points.

Increases the stats of the Ghost form with every level.
Locust Swarm

Creates a swarm of 20 angry locusts that bite and tear at nearby enemy units. As they chew the enemy flesh, they convert it into a substance that restores 75 % of the caused damage as hit points to the hero when they return.
Lasts 180 seconds.

Increases the converted hit points and number of locusts with every level.
Spell Shield

Blocks a negative spell that an enemy casts on the Hero once every 40 seconds.

Decreases the cooldown with every level.
Charm

Takes permanent control of a target enemy unit.
Charm cannot be used on Heroes, or creeps above level 6. When Charm is being used on workers, they will die instantly.

Increases the maximum creep level with every level.
Big Bad Voodoo

Turns all friendly units invulnerable in an area around the hero.
The hero does not turn invulnerable.
Lasts 20 seconds.

Increases the duration with every level.
Tinker

Changes the class to Tinker:
- Repair
- Damage
- Mechanical units
Tinker
Repair

Repairs mechanical units and structures at the cost of resources with a ratio of 35 %. Can repair the hero if the hero is mechanical.

Increases the ratio of repaired hit points with every level.
Harvest

Summons 1 Bundle of Lumbers and Gold Coins.

Increases the charges of the items with every level.
Demolish

Causes attacks to do 2 times normal damage to buildings and mechanical units.

Increases the damage factor with every level.
Pocket Factory

Creates a factory which automatically constructs Clockwerk Goblins. These artificial Goblins, in addition to being potent attackers, explode upon death, causing damage to nearby enemy units.

Increases the stats of the factory and Clockwerk Goblins and the caused damage by explosions with every level.
Summon Explosive Barrel

Summons an explosive barrel which explodes when manually triggered. This causes 700 damage to nearby enemy ground units.

Increases the caused damage with every level.
Repair Aura

Increases the life regeneration rate by 50 % of nearby friendly mechanical units.

Increases the regeneration bonus with every level.
Jetpack

Uses the Jetpack to fly to another revealed location with a maximum distance of 600 causing 45 damage to enemy nearby ground units on landing.

Increases the maximum distance and damage with every level.
Cluster Rockets

Bombards an area with rockets, dealing up to 99999 damage to nearby enemy units with 6 rockets and stunning them for 1 second.

Increases the damage amount and missile count with every level.
Engineering Upgrade

Enhances other hero ability levels with each level learned. Use chat command '-n' to see all possible spells which can be improved.

Also gives bonus damage and increases the hero's movement speed.

Increases the ability enhancement, bonus damage amount and movement speed with every level.
Transmute

Kills a target enemy unit instantly, transforming it into 30% gold and 30% lumber which is added to your available resources!
Transmute pierces spell immunity, but cannot be used on Heroes, or creeps above level 6.

Increases the received gold, lumber and maximum creep level with every level.
Robo-Goblin

Transforms the hero into a Robo-Goblin, a powerful armored form that has the following traits:

- 1 bonus Strength and 1 bonus armor.
- Use of the Demolish ability, which grants bonus damage against buildings.
- The hero becomes mechanical, rendering him immune to most forms of stun, most offensive spells, and several beneficial spells.

Increases the bonus strength and armor with every level.
